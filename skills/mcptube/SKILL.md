# mcptube Skill

## Usage

mcptube transforms YouTube videos into a persistent, structured knowledge base. Use it when you need to:

- Ingest YouTube videos and extract transcripts + visual frames
- Build a compounding wiki knowledge base from video content
- Search or ask questions over ingested video knowledge
- Generate illustrated reports across one or more videos
- Discover and cluster YouTube videos on a topic

## Examples

- `mcptube add "https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=..."` — ingest a video
- `mcptube ask "What is RLHF?"` — query the knowledge base
- `mcptube wiki toc` — view all knowledge pages
- `mcptube discover "prompt engineering"` — find videos on a topic
